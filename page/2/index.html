One experience on changing rom on RedMi/XiaoMi and Play Store Installation

Posted on 2017-11-23 | In Others

Change rom to the XiaoMi phone easily. Here are the steps.

  1. Prepare the usb or type c for your device, then connect it with your computer
  2. Shutdown your device and then press the power-off button and volume down key at the same time, after a while you will see the fastboot mode.
  3. Download the miflash-unlock tool to unlock your device. Then install it and open, following the prompts you will unlock the device.
